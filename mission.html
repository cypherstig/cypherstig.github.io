Mission

Bitcoin is a peaceful revolution, proving the power of voluntary cooperation in the fight for freedom.

Cypherstig is contantly devouring content produced by Bitcoin thought leaders across the globe to bring you comprehensive educational materials and unique perspectives, fueling a curious, more knowledgable community.

Collectively, we now wield the most powerful tool for human liberation in the history of mankind. Cypherstig's mission is to arm Bitcoin learners and teachers with the knowledge and passion necessary to spread sound money through every community being oppressed by corrupt authoritarian regimes leeching power from the centralized fiat currencies which they control.

We hope our content serves as a valuable resource during your journey down the Bitcoin rabbit hole. Peer-to-peer, hand-in-hand, we can't be stopped when working together, so please consider sharing our content with loved ones to have them join you on this life-changing adventure.
